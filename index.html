About ArcANE

Our Mission

ArcANE (Archaeology: Accessibility, Networking, Engagement) is an open-access project developed by students of archaeology and ancient history for professionals, students, and hobbyists worldwide.

We aim to create a community-driven resource pool that is easily accessible by any person across the globe, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing in the field of archaeology.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Making archaeological knowledge available to everyone
  • Networking: Connecting professionals, students, and enthusiasts
  • Engagement: Fostering active participation in archaeological discourse
  • Open Access: Removing barriers to archaeological information

Our Team

ArcANE is developed and maintained by a dedicated team of archaeology and ancient history students from universities around the world. Our diverse backgrounds bring different perspectives and expertise to the platform.
